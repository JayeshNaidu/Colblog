About Colblog

This is what it does.

There’s communication everywhere be it online or offline in the office or school or college. When it comes to college there’s communication between student and teachers as well as between staff and teachers. We also know that students face difficulty expressing their thoughts to the teachers as well as the management. it could be about the portions running fast or anything in the classroom causing discomfort or it could be a idea suggested by anyone.

To solve this issue we can use a blog that actually let everyone pour their thoughts, this blog only belongs to the people of the college. These posts could be about anything but however it can be read by everyone registered to the blog. A blog gives you the chance to express what you feel and at some point, other internet users can be inspired by the same.

This way Management knows what changes they need to make to solve their problems and teachers understand how their classes are, therefore students improve their quality of education.
